## ✈️ Steps Forward
In Chinese, highlights are called **"風光 (feng guang)"**, and lines are called **"鋒 (feng)"**.

With "風" and "鋒" sharing **the exact same pronunciation**,
replace "風" with "鋒" gives "鋒光", **meaning highlights of lines.**

In English, __lines & shines rhyme__, so why not 😎

I think these are why I created LinesShines on top of
[my past local NFL plots era](https://github.com/StarsExpress/LinesShines/tree/v0.0-beginning).
